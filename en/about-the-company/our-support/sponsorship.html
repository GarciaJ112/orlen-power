Sponsorship

For years, the engagement in professional and amateur sports sponsoring, patronage on major cultural events and endorsement of valuable community initiatives have been a key element of our corporate identity. Such actions help us not only strengthen reputation of ORLEN brand, but express responsibility towards our stakeholders.
