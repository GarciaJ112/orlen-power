Personal data

At ORLEN S.A. personal data is processed in accordance with Regulation (EU) No. 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of April 27th 2016 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC (General Data Protection Regulation, “GDPR”) and in accordance with Polish legislation on the processing of personal data.

THE DATA CONTROLLER

The data controller of your personal data is Polski Koncert Naftowy ORLEN S.A. with its registered office at ul. Chemików 7, Płock, Poland  (hereinafter referred to as „ORLEN S.A.”). Contact phone numbers to the controller: (24) 256 00 00, (24) 365 00 00, (22) 778 00 00.

THE DATA PROTECTION OFFICER

You can contact the Data Protection Officer in ORLEN S.A. by e-mail to: daneosobowe@orlen.ltd. You can also contact the Data Protection Officer in writing to the address of the registered office of ORLEN S.A., indicated in item 1, with additional information “Inspektor Ochrony Danych” (Data Protection Officer).

DATA TYPES AND PROCESSING PURPOSES

In connection with e-Receipt service we process your personal data related to making purchases, i.e .:

  •  information about the place where the purchases were made - location,
  •  date and time of issuing the receipt (information about the date and time of making purchases),
  • information purchased goods or services (quantity or number of goods or services purchased, their price, and the amount spent on individual goods and services and the total amount spent, receipt numer),
  • transaction and client identifier received from Powszechna Kasa Oszczędności Bank Polski (hereinafter referred to as : „PKO BP”).

We process your personal data in order to generate electronic receipts and transfer them to PKO BP, so that you can use the e-Receipt service.

LEGAL GROUNDS FOR THE PROCESSING

The legal basis for the processing by ORLEN S.A. Your personal data for the purpose indicated in item 3 above is the legitimate interest of ORLEN S.A. (in accordance with Article 6 (1) (f) of the GDPR) - participation in an innovative project consisting in generating electronic receipts, which is an added value for you and additional confirmation of the transaction between you and ORLEN S.A.

DATA RECIPIENTS

Your personal data will be disclosed by ORLEN S.A. to the entity providing the e-Receipt service, i.e. to the PKO BP.

Your personal data may be disclosed by ORLEN S.A. to entities cooperating with it (data recipients), in particular entities providing IT services, sale of goods and services, settlement of receivables

DATA RECEIVED FROM THIRD PARTIES

As part of the e-Receipt service, ORLEN S.A. receives from PKO BP an individual transaction and customer ID generated by PKO BP for you.

PERIOD OF PERSONAL DATA PROCESSING

Your personal data are processed until the mutual claims expire, not less than for the period stipulated by law.

RIGHTS RELATED TO DATA PROCESSING

In connection with the processing of personal data, you are entitled to:

  • the right to access the content of your data,
  • the right to require rectification of your personal data,
  • the right to request the deletion of personal data or limitation of data processing.

In order to exercise these rights, please contact us or our data protection officer (contact details in item 1 and 2 above).

THE RIGHT TO OBJECT

You have the right to object to the disclosure of your data by ORLEN S.A. to the bank PKO BP. Raising an objection means resignation from the e-Receipt service provided by PKO BP.

We kindly inform you, that the role of ORLEN S.A. in the e-Paragon service it consists solely in generating an e-Paragon and transfer e-Paragon to PKO BP.

To object to the disclosure of data by ORLEN S.A. to the PKO BP, you should resign from the e-Receipt service. To cancel e-Paragon service, check the appropriate option in the PKO BP electronic banking system. Details on resignation from the e-Receipt service can be found in the Regulations of the pilot service of the "e-Receipt for individual clients of PKO Bank Polski SA" available at www.pkobp.pl \ e-receipt. In this case, you can also call the Helpline of PKO Bank Polski.

RIGHT TO FILE A COMPLAINT WITH A SUPERVISORY AUTHORITY

You have the right to file a complaint to the President of the Personal Data Protection Office.

I              Personal data controllers

The controllers of your personal data are: ORLEN S.A. with its registered office in Płock, ul. Chemików 7 and the company of ORLEN capital group or another entity of ORLEN capital group (the list of entity is annexed as Attachment No. 2 to the Regulations of the Company Social Activity)

The controllers jointly manage your personal data on the basis of the Agreement on co-administration of personal data which is available on the website www.orlen.pl in the "Responsible Business - Personal Data" tab.

II            Common arrangements between controllers

Under Agreement on co-administration of personal data, the controllers have jointly agreed on the responsibilities for fulfilling the obligations arising from GDPR, in particular:

a)    Each Administrator, at the time of collecting your personal data for purposes related to the Social Benefits Fund, is responsible for the fulfillment information obligation regarding the processing of your personal data,

b)    Each Administrator, in terms of collecting personal data, is responsible for enabling you to exercise your rights related to the processing of your personal data,

c)    Regardless of the arrangements in point b above, you may submit the right to request access to your personal data to ORLEN S.A. In this case, ORLEN S.A. will provide your request to the competent Controller who will implement it

III           Contact point

Each of the Controller has appointed Data Protection Officer or GDPR Coordinator. The Controllers have established a common contact point, through which you can contact us regarding the protection of your personal data:

a)    e-mail address: daneosobowe.sgk@orlen.ltd,

b)    postal address: ORLEN S.A., Data Protection Officer (Inspektor Ochrony Danych), ul. Chemików 7, 09-411 Płock,

c)    ORLEN S.A. undertakes to service the above-mentioned contact points, which does not exclude the right to contact the competent Controller.

IV          Purposes of processing

Your personal data are processed for the following purposes:

a)    You benefit from social benefits or loan guarantees offered by the Social Benefits Fund at ORLEN S.A. and entities that have concluded the Agreement on joint social activity,

b)    Archives which are the implementation of the law,

c)    Handle, pursue and defence of claims,

d)    Satisfaction survey of people using the benefits of the Social Benefits Fund, determining the quality of service and the level of satisfaction with the benefits offered.

V           The legal grounds for the processing

The legal grounds for processing by the Controllers of your personal data for the purposes defined in paragraph IV above include:

a)    fulfilment of legal obligations (in compliance Article 6(1)(c) of the GDPR), in connection with your use of social benefits offered by the Social Benefits Fund at ORLEN S.A. and the companies of ORLEN capital group under the Act on Employee Benefit Fund (on condition of using these benefits),

b)    fulfilment of legal obligations (in compliance Article 6(1)(c) of the GDPR), for archiving purposes,

c)    implement the legitimate interest (in compliance Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR) for the purpose of handling, investigating and defending in the event of mutual claims,

d)    implement the legitimate interest (in compliance Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR) for the purpose of testing the satisfaction of people using the benefits of the Company Social Benefits Fund,

e)    the data of the guarantors will be processed in order to guarantee the loan taken by the borrower, in compliance Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR and Article 6(1)(c) of the GDPR in connection with a loan agreement concluded in accordance with the applicable Regulations of the Company Social Activity.

VI         Period of personal data processing

Your personal data will be processed for the period of using the benefits from the Social Benefits Fund or the period required by law in connection with the guarantee and for the period stipulated by law, not less than until the expiry of mutual claims.

VII         Data recipients

Your personal data may be disclosed by Controllers to entities cooperating with them (data recipients), in particular:

a)    entities and bodies authorized to process this data on the basis of legal provisions,

b)    entities processing personal data at the request of the Controller, for the purpose of handling correspondence as well as the implementation and service of social benefits in ORLEN and entities that have concluded the Agreement on joint social activity,

c)    members of the Social Committee referred to the Regulations of the Company Social Activity in the scope of its operation and decision-making,

d)    The Senior Club, authorized Members of the Senior Club Councils - in terms of activities carried out by them, i.e. organization of meetings and trips

VIII         Information Information on the requirement to provide data

The obligation to provide personal data results in particular from the provisions of law, i.e. the Act on company social benefits fund.

IX          Rights of data subjects

In connection with the processing of personal data, you are entitled to:

a)    the right to access the content of your data,

b)    the right to require rectification of your personal data,

c)    the right to require erasure of your personal data,

d)    the right to limit processing,

e)    the right to object due to a special situation - in cases where the data are processed on the basis of a legitimate interest,

f)     the right to data portability, i.e. the right to receive from Controller your personal data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format. You may send the received data to another controller or request Controller to send your data to another controller. However, this request may only be met by Controller where technically feasible. The right to data portability may be only exercised with regard to the data processed under an agreement concluded with you,

You may file a complaint to the President of the Personal Data Protection Office.

X            Exercise of your rights

To use the above rights, please contact the Administrator in the manner indicated in paragraph  IV above.
