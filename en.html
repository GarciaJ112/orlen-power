BALTIC POWER

The largest green energy project in Central Europe

Baltic Power, a joint venture od ORLEN and Northland Power has reached the most advanced  develompent phase among all offshore wind projects in Poland and the only one to obtain FID. At the moment its onshore infrastructure and key packages are being constructed, while the offshore installation works are up to set out in the beginning of 2025. 76 turbines of 15 MW capacity will be able to cover approx. 3% of Poland’s electricity demand, which stands for the daily needs of 1,5 M households.

ORLEN and Northland Power Commence Construction of Poland’s First Offshore Wind Farm

Fleet of 11 specialised vessels has mobilised to begin installing the foundations for Poland’s first offshore wind farm in the Baltic Sea. This stage has already seen the successful installation of the first two of a total of 78 monopiles – towering 100-metre steel structures that will support 15 MW wind turbines.
